Our Team

Meet the Group 3 students from Conestoga College dedicated to exploring the intersection of technology and sustainability.

This website was created collaboratively by Group 3 students for the Conestoga College English Technical Communication project. Our diverse team has combined research, technical expertise, and project coordination to deliver a comprehensive resource on the role of Computer Science in mitigating climate change.

Group Collaboration Process

The group worked collaboratively through shared online tools, ensuring that all members contributed to both the initial planning and the final development of the project. Clear communication was used to effectively divide tasks and review ongoing progress. Through regular discussion and a spirit of cooperation, the group successfully resolved any issues and integrated individual sections into a cohesive final website.

Our Approach to Teamwork

  • Shared planning and research responsibilities
  • Collaborative editing through online tools
  • Regular communication and progress updates
  • Peer review before final submission
